# Social Media & Professional Networking Etiquette

###  The Do's
* **Keep Profiles Professional:** Use a high-quality headshot for LinkedIn and GitHub.
* **Verify Information:** Before sharing a post, ensure the news or data is from a credible source to avoid spreading misinformation.
* **Respect Privacy:** Always ask for permission before tagging others in photos or sharing screenshots of private conversations.
* **Engage Meaningfully:** Comment on industry-related posts with insightful thoughts rather than just "Great post!"
* **Update Your Bio:** Ensure your current skills, branch, and academic goals are clearly stated.

###  The Don'ts
* **Don't Overshare:** Avoid posting highly personal or controversial opinions on professional platforms like LinkedIn.
* **Don't Tag Excessively:** Avoid tagging 20+ people in a post just to get views; it is seen as spam.
* **Don't Ignore DMs:** If someone reaches out professionally, provide a polite response, even if you are not interested in the opportunity.
* **Don't Plagiarize Content:** Always give credit when sharing someone else's work, code, or research findings.
* **Don't Use Weak Passwords:** Protect your professional identity by using strong, unique passwords for every social platform.
